Our Story

Born from Research,
Driven by Wanderlust

Wanderers Itinerary was born from two things — a love for travel and a mind trained for research. With an academic background in Biotechnology and experience in the research field, detailed analysis, planning, and problem-solving have always been second nature.

Over time, these skills naturally found their way into travel planning — researching destinations deeply, studying routes, logistics, and timing, and creating clear, practical day-by-day itineraries.

What started as planning personal trips soon turned into helping others who wanted to travel independently but didn't have the time to research everything themselves.

Our itineraries cost less than 1% of your total trip cost — but save you hours of research and planning. That's the most efficient travel investment you'll make.

The Case for Independence

Why DIY Travel
Works Better

One belief drives this platform: travel doesn't have to be expensive. When planned well and in advance, international travel can be surprisingly affordable. DIY travel also gives you something traditional tours rarely do — true freedom.

Except for senior citizens who may prefer structured tours for comfort, most travellers can easily navigate countries independently with the right guidance. That's exactly where we come in.

  • Travel at your own pace — no fixed schedules, no rushing
  • Skip places you are not interested in, spend more time where you are
  • Add experiences you truly enjoy — local markets, hidden cafes, offbeat trails
  • Save significantly compared to fixed tour packages
  • Experience destinations authentically, not just as a tourist

What's Inside
Each Itinerary

  • Day-wise travel plan with clear daily schedules
  • Exact transport routes — train, metro, bus, ferry — with Google Maps links
  • Attraction planning for each day to cover places efficiently
  • Approximate travel costs to help you budget your trip
  • Budget-friendly hotel recommendations in prime locations with booking links
  • Veg, Jain, and Vegan-friendly restaurant suggestions with direct links
  • Local travel tips to help you navigate the destination easily
  • Time-efficient route planning — more exploring, less figuring out
  • Estimated total trip cost for complete financial planning
  • Essential apps to download before the trip for smoother travel
  • Optional activities and alternatives for flexible customisation
